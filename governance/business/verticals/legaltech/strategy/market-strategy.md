# Court Lens — Market Strategy

> Canadian-first AI legal analytics platform analyzing 500K+ CanLII decisions for case outcome prediction, judicial tendency analysis, and litigation optimization.

---

## Total Addressable Market

| Segment | Value | Description |
|---------|-------|-------------|
| Global Legaltech | $17B | Full legal technology market (2024) |
| Canadian Legal Analytics | $2.5B | Analytics, research, and AI tools for Canadian legal market |
| Target SOM (2030) | $300K | Initial penetration via Ontario litigators |

### Market Segmentation by Practice Area

- **Litigation (Civil)** — Largest segment, 40% of addressable market, highest prediction value
- **Family Law** — High volume, strong demand for outcome prediction and settlement ranges
- **Criminal Defence** — Sentencing prediction, judicial tendency analysis critical
- **Immigration** — Tribunal decisions, approval rate analytics
- **Corporate/Commercial** — Contract disputes, regulatory proceedings

### Market Segmentation by Firm Size

- Solo Practitioners: 22,000+ in Ontario alone — high volume, price-sensitive
- Small Firms (2-10 lawyers): 4,500+ firms — team collaboration needs
- Mid-Size Firms (11-50): 800+ firms — enterprise features, API access
- Large/Bay Street Firms (50+): 150+ firms — premium analytics, custom reports

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

| Competitor | Focus | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|-------|-----------|------------|
| Lex Machina | US litigation analytics | Deep US federal data, established brand | No Canadian data, US-only courts |
| Premonition.ai | Global win rate analytics | Broad coverage | Shallow analytics, accuracy concerns |
| Blue J Legal | Canadian tax prediction | Strong AI, Canadian-specific | Tax-only, narrow practice area |
| ROSS Intelligence | Legal research AI | NLP innovation | Defunct (2021), IP sold |

### Adjacent Competitors

| Competitor | Category | Overlap |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| Clio | Practice management | Client data, workflows — not analytics |
| LexisNexis / Westlaw | Legal research | Case database — not predictive analytics |
| Kira Systems | Contract analysis | Document review — different use case |
| Diligen | Due diligence | M&A focused — no litigation prediction |

### Court Lens Differentiation

1. **Only Canadian-specific AI prediction platform** — competitors are US-focused or defunct
2. **500K+ CanLII corpus** with continuous ingestion pipeline
3. **Bilingual** (English/French) — critical for Quebec market
4. **Judicial tendency profiles** for 2,000+ Canadian judges
5. **pgVector semantic search** — superior precedent discovery vs keyword-only competitors

## Go-to-Market Playbook

### Phase 1: Ontario (2025-2026)

- **Target**: Law Society of Ontario — 55,000 licensed lawyers
- **Entry**: Civil litigation practitioners (highest prediction value)
- **Channels**: LSO-sponsored CLE (Continuing Legal Education) workshops, OBA conferences
- **Goal**: 200 active subscribers, $240K ARR

### Phase 2: Pan-Canadian (2026-2027)

- **Target**: Canadian Bar Association — 36,000+ members
- **Expand**: BC (LSBC), Alberta (LSA), Quebec (Barreau du Québec)
- **Channels**: CBA national conference, provincial bar partnerships, law school programs
- **Goal**: 800 active subscribers, $960K ARR

### Phase 3: Common Law Jurisdictions (2028+)

- **Target**: UK (BAILII corpus), Australia (AustLII), India (Indian Kanoon)
- **Strategy**: Adapt CanLII pipeline to sister LII databases
- **Channels**: ILTACON, Legal Geek London, partnership with LII network
- **Goal**: International expansion, $2M+ ARR

## Pricing Strategy

| Tier | Price | Features | Target |
|------|-------|----------|--------|
| Solo Practitioner | $99/mo | 50 predictions/mo, basic search, 5 judge profiles | Solo lawyers |
| Small Firm | $299/mo | 200 predictions/mo, full search, team dashboard, 50 judge profiles | 2-10 lawyer firms |
| Large Firm | $699/mo | Unlimited predictions, API access, custom reports, opposing counsel analytics | 11-50 lawyer firms |
| Enterprise | $1,500+/mo | White-label, dedicated support, custom models, bulk API | Bay Street / national firms |

### Pricing Rationale
- Solo at $99/mo: below typical Westlaw/LexisNexis research subscription — easy upsell
- Enterprise at $1,500/mo: fraction of a junior associate's hourly rate — clear ROI
- All tiers include 14-day free trial with sample predictions on user's practice area

## Customer Acquisition Strategy

### Channels (by priority)
1. **CLE Workshops** — Present prediction accuracy data, earn CLE credits for attendees
2. **Legal Conferences** — LTNY (LegalTech New York), ILTACON, OBA Institute — demo booth + speaking slots
3. **Law School Pilots** — Free academic tier → graduating students bring Court Lens to firms
4. **Content Marketing** — Monthly judicial tendency reports, practice area outcome analyses
5. **Referral Program** — 2 months free for referring lawyer, 1 month free for new subscriber

### CAC Optimization
- Target CAC: $150 (current)
- CLE workshop CAC: ~$80 (warm leads, high conversion)
- Conference CAC: ~$250 (broad awareness, lower conversion)
- Law school pipeline: ~$0 CAC (organic adoption post-graduation)
- Content/SEO: ~$50 CAC (compound returns over time)
