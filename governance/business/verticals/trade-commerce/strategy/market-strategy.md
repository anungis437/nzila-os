## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Focus | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|-------|-----------|------------|
| Flexport | Full-service digital freight forwarding | End-to-end logistics, strong brand | Expensive, US-centric, not SME-friendly |
| Freightos | Online freight marketplace | Rate transparency, marketplace model | No compliance/documentation tools |
| project44 | Visibility platform | Real-time tracking, enterprise clients | Tracking only — no booking or compliance |
| Descartes | Customs & compliance | Dominant in Canadian customs, EDI | Legacy UX, expensive, enterprise-only |
| Cargowise | TMS/freight management | Comprehensive, global | Complex, requires implementation partner |

### Trade OS Differentiation
1. **Multi-carrier rate shopping + compliance** in one platform (vs separate tools)
2. **SME-accessible** pricing ($299/mo vs $2,000+/mo for Descartes/Cargowise)
3. **USMCA rules of origin** calculator (niche but critical for Canada-US trade)
4. **Africa corridor expertise** — unique positioning no competitor offers

## Go-to-Market Playbook

### Phase 1: Ontario Base (2025–2026)
- **Target**: Ontario freight forwarders, customs brokers (CSCB — 5,000 members)
- **Entry**: USMCA compliance tools (highest pain point for Canadian exporters)
- **Channels**: CSCB conference, CIFFA events, EDC export workshops
- **Goal**: 150 combined subscribers, $300K ARR

### Phase 2: Pan-Canadian + USMCA (2026–2027)
- **Target**: National expansion, US-Canada corridor optimization
- **Strategy**: Cross-border expertise, multi-modal (truck + rail + air + ocean)
- **Channels**: Canadian Chamber of Commerce, provincial trade councils, BDC partnerships
- **Goal**: 500 subscribers, $900K ARR

### Phase 3: Africa Corridors (2028+)
- **Target**: Canada–Africa trade (leveraging Nzila's diaspora network presence)
- **Strategy**: Agricultural exports (CORA synergy), manufactured goods, mining supplies
- **Channels**: Africa–Canada trade associations, Nzila vertical cross-referrals
- **Goal**: 800+ subscribers, $1.5M+ ARR

## Partnership Strategy

### Customs & Logistics
- **CSCB** (Canadian Society of Customs Brokers) — 5,000 members, endorsed tool
- **CIFFA** (Canadian International Freight Forwarders Association) — member integration
- **Carrier integrations**: Canada Post, UPS, FedEx, Purolator, Maersk, MSC, CMA CGM

### Government & Trade
- **EDC** (Export Development Canada) — referral partner for SME exporters
- **BDC** (Business Development Bank of Canada) — trade financing integration
- **Global Affairs Canada** — trade commissioner service referrals
- **Provincial trade councils**: Ontario, BC, Alberta, Quebec

### Technology
- **Accounting platforms**: QuickBooks, Xero — invoice sync with eEXPORTS
- **E-commerce**: Shopify, WooCommerce — Shop Quoter integration
- **ERP**: NetSuite, SAP Business One — enterprise data sync
