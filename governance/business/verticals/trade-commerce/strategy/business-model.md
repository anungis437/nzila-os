# Trade & Commerce — Business Model

> Revenue model, unit economics, and financial projections for Trade OS, eEXPORTS, and Shop Quoter.

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Trade OS — Freight & Logistics (50% of Vertical Revenue)

| Stream | Pricing | Description |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| SaaS Subscription | $299–$1,499/mo | Tiered by shipment volume and carrier access |
| Per-Shipment Fee | $2–$10 | Applied on top of subscription, scales with volume |
| Carrier Placement | 1–3% override | Revenue share from carriers on booked shipments |
| API Access | $0.10–$1.00/call | Third-party integrations, partner access |

### 2. eEXPORTS — Documentation & Compliance (30% of Vertical Revenue)

| Stream | Pricing | Description |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| SaaS Subscription | $99–$499/mo | Tiered by document volume and compliance features |
| Per-Document Fee | $1–$5 | Commercial invoices, certificates of origin |
| HS Code Classification | $0.50/query | AI-assisted classification |
| Customs Broker Portal | $299/mo | Broker-specific features, client management |

### 3. Shop Quoter — Retail & POS (20% of Vertical Revenue)

| Stream | Pricing | Description |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| SaaS Subscription | $49–$199/mo | Tiered by product count and POS terminals |
| Transaction Fee | 0.5% | On POS transactions beyond included volume |
| Marketplace Fee | 3% | On supplier marketplace transactions |
| Shopify/WooCommerce Sync | $29/mo add-on | E-commerce platform integration |

## Unit Economics

### Per-Platform Breakdown

| Metric | Trade OS | eEXPORTS | Shop Quoter |
|--------|----------|----------|-------------|
| LTV | $12,000 | $4,800 | $3,600 |
| CAC | $180 | $120 | $80 |
| LTV/CAC | 67x | 40x | 45x |
| Monthly ARPU | $500 | $200 | $100 |
| Avg Retention | 24 months | 24 months | 36 months |
| Payback Period | < 1 month | < 1 month | < 1 month |
| Monthly Churn | 3% | 3.5% | 2% |
| Gross Margin | 75% | 82% | 85% |

### Blended Vertical Economics
- **Blended LTV**: $7,200 (weighted by subscriber mix)
- **Blended CAC**: $130 (weighted)
- **Blended LTV/CAC**: 55x
- **Target Blended ARPU**: $310/mo

### Cross-Sell Economics
The 3-platform funnel creates exceptional unit economics:
- Shop Quoter → eEXPORTS upgrade: 15% of Shop Quoter users export → $0 marginal CAC
- eEXPORTS → Trade OS upgrade: 25% of exporters adopt freight tools → $0 marginal CAC
- Cross-platform users have 2.5x higher LTV and 40% lower churn

## Cost Structure

### Variable Costs
- Carrier API calls: $0.01–$0.05 per rate query
- Azure OpenAI (HS classification): $0.02 per classification
- Document generation: $0.005 per PDF
- Stripe processing (Shop Quoter): 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction

### Fixed Monthly Costs (at scale)

| Category | Monthly Cost | % Revenue |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Azure Infrastructure | $2,500–$6,000 | 3–6% |
| Engineering Team | $20,000 | 20–25% |
| Carrier Integration Maintenance | $3,000 | 3–5% |
| Sales & Marketing | $8,000 | 8–12% |
| Customer Support | $5,000 | 5–8% |
| Compliance/Legal | $2,000 | 2–4% |
| **Total** | **$40,500–$44,000** | **45–55%** |

### Path to Profitability
- Breakeven: ~$50K MRR (~160 subscribers at $310 ARPU)
- Target breakeven: Q4 2025
- Post-breakeven gross margin: 75–80%
