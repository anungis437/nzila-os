1. Purpose

This document outlines how Memora implements lightweight, fatigue-aware gamification to promote consistent use, cognitive engagement, and emotional affirmation without pressure.

All elements are:

  • Non-competitive, non-coercive, and non-diagnostic
  • Designed to reinforce effort, not performance
  • Opt-in, consent-aware, and accessible by design
  • Governed by Companion tone, streak logic, and user preferences
