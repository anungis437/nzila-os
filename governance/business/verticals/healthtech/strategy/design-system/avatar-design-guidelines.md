### **1.2 Human Avatars**

**Overview:**

These avatars should also be designed to provide **comfort** and **trust**, reflecting Nzila's core principles of **care** and **empathy**.

### **Key Elements:**

- **Age Representation**:
- Design avatars for **various age groups**, ensuring each age group has **distinct characteristics** appropriate for their stage of life (e.g., simplified features for children, more mature designs for elderly users).
- **Accessibility Features**:
- Ensure that **user preferences** can be incorporated into avatar customization (e.g., different skin tones, clothing choices, etc.).

### **1.3 Animal Avatars**

**Overview:**

Animal avatars are intended to be **playful**, **engaging**, and **emotionally supportive**, ideal for users who respond well to non-human interaction or are in need of a lighter, more comforting presence. These avatars can also serve in a **gamification context**, where the goal is to engage users through more **dynamic** and **interactive** methods.

### **Key Elements:**

- **Animal Types**:
- Include a variety of animal avatars, such as **dogs**, **cats**, **birds**, and **mythical creatures** (e.g., dragons, unicorns) that can appeal to different user types.
- **Emotional Impact**: Animals should evoke emotions such as **comfort**, **joy**, or **encouragement**, providing emotional support in a way human avatars may not.
- **Animal Features**:
- Avatars should include **adjustable features** (e.g., fur color, **tail movement**, **ears positioning**) to make them feel more personalized.
- Include non-verbal cues that indicate **emotion** (e.g., wagging tail, perked ears) to enhance **interaction**.
- **Engagement in Gamification**:
- Animal avatars should have the ability to **engage** with users in a **playful, positive manner**, encouraging completion of tasks or offering praise in a **non-judgmental** way.

### **Customization Options**:

- **Fur/Color**: Users can choose between different **fur textures** and **colors** to personalize their avatar.
- **Behavioral Features**: Customize **tail movement**, **ear positioning**, and other **non-verbal** cues to reflect **emotional state**.
