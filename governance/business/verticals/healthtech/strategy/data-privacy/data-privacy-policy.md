# Data Privacy Policy

**Owner:** Aubert

### **1. Introduction**

This **Data Privacy Policy** outlines **Nzila Ventures'** commitment to protecting the personal data and privacy of its employees, customers, contractors, partners, and other stakeholders. The policy is designed to comply with global data protection laws and regulations, including the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, the **California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)**, and other applicable privacy laws.

This policy governs the collection, use, storage, sharing, and disposal of personal data, ensuring that **Nzila Ventures** takes appropriate measures to safeguard privacy rights while maintaining the integrity and confidentiality of personal information.

---

### **2. Purpose of the Policy**

The purpose of this **Data Privacy Policy** is to ensure:
- Compliance with all **data protection laws and regulations**.
- Transparency in how **Nzila Ventures** collects, processes, stores, and shares personal data.
- The protection of individuals' **privacy rights** and the safeguarding of their personal data.
- The establishment of a framework for managing privacy risks in all business operations.

---

### **3. Scope**

This policy applies to:
- **Personal data** of employees, customers, contractors, and any other individuals whose data is processed by **Nzila Ventures**.
- All **data collection**, **processing**, **storage**, and **disposal** activities undertaken by the company.
- **All departments**, including HR, marketing, product development, sales, and customer service, which handle personal data.

This policy is aligned with international standards, including **GDPR**, **CCPA**, **ISO/IEC 27001**, and **NIST Cybersecurity Framework**, to ensure a consistent, secure, and compliant approach to data privacy.

---

### **4. Data Privacy Principles**

The **Data Privacy Policy** is grounded in the following principles, which are aligned with the requirements of **GDPR** and other global privacy frameworks:

| **Principle** | **Description** | **Framework Alignment** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency** | Personal data will be collected and processed lawfully, fairly, and in a transparent manner. | **GDPR Article 5(1)(a)** |
| **Purpose Limitation** | Personal data will be collected for specified, legitimate purposes and not processed for incompatible purposes. | **GDPR Article 5(1)(b)** |
| **Data Minimization** | Only personal data that is necessary for the purposes of processing will be collected. | **GDPR Article 5(1)(c)** |
| **Accuracy** | Personal data will be kept accurate and, where necessary, up to date. | **GDPR Article 5(1)(d)** |
| **Storage Limitation** | Personal data will be kept in a form that allows identification of data subjects for no longer than necessary. | **GDPR Article 5(1)(e)** |
| **Integrity and Confidentiality** | Personal data will be processed in a way that ensures appropriate security, including protection against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage. | **GDPR Article 5(1)(f)** |
| **Accountability** | Nzila Ventures will be accountable for complying with the above principles and will implement appropriate measures to demonstrate compliance. | **GDPR Article 5(2)** |

---

### **5. Data Collection**

Nzila Ventures collects personal data from various sources, including but not limited to:
- **Direct interactions** with individuals (e.g., website forms, job applications, customer inquiries).
- **Third-party sources** (e.g., partners, service providers).
- **Publicly available sources**, as legally permissible.

### **Types of Data Collected**:

- **Personal Identifiable Information (PII)**: Name, contact details, address, date of birth, etc.
- **Sensitive Data** (only when applicable and with explicit consent): Health data, financial information, etc.
- **Usage Data**: IP address, browsing activity, cookies, etc.

---

### **6. Data Use and Purpose**

Nzila Ventures processes personal data for the following purposes:

| **Purpose** | **Data Use** |
| --- | --- |
| **Contractual Obligations** | Processing data to fulfill contracts and agreements (e.g., service delivery, employment contracts). |
| **Customer Service and Support** | To respond to inquiries, provide support, and maintain client relationships. |
| **Marketing and Promotions** | To communicate with customers about products, services, and offers, based on consent. |
| **Compliance and Legal Obligations** | To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and contracts (e.g., tax, accounting, employment law). |
| **Employee and HR Management** | Managing employee records, payroll, benefits, performance, and legal compliance. |

---

### **7. Data Sharing and Disclosure**

**Nzila Ventures** may share personal data in the following circumstances:
- **Service Providers**: With trusted third parties who assist with business operations (e.g., cloud storage, IT services), provided they adhere to data protection standards.
- **Legal Obligations**: To comply with legal requirements, such as responding to court orders, subpoenas, or requests from government authorities.
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, personal data may be transferred to the new entity.

No personal data will be sold or rented to third parties.

---

### **8. Data Security**

Nzila Ventures implements technical, administrative, and physical security measures to protect personal data, including:
- **Encryption**: Sensitive data will be encrypted both at rest and in transit.
- **Access Control**: Only authorized personnel have access to personal data, with access controlled based on need-to-know principles.
- **Security Monitoring**: Regular monitoring and auditing of data systems to detect and prevent unauthorized access or breaches.

**Framework Alignment**:
- **ISO/IEC 27001**: Information Security Management System (ISMS)
- **NIST CSF**: Protect and Detect functions

---

### **9. Data Retention**

Personal data will be retained only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law. Once personal data is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

---

### **10. Rights of Data Subjects**

Under **GDPR** and other applicable privacy laws, individuals have the following rights regarding their personal data:

| **Right** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| **Right to Access** | Individuals may request a copy of their personal data held by **Nzila Ventures**. |
| **Right to Rectification** | Individuals may request corrections or updates to inaccurate or incomplete personal data. |
| **Right to Erasure** (Right to be Forgotten) | Individuals may request the deletion of their personal data, subject to certain conditions. |
| **Right to Restriction of Processing** | Individuals may request to restrict processing of their personal data in certain circumstances. |
| **Right to Data Portability** | Individuals may request a copy of their personal data in a structured, machine-readable format. |
| **Right to Object** | Individuals may object to the processing of their personal data in certain cases, including for marketing. |
| **Right to Withdraw Consent** | Individuals may withdraw consent at any time for data processing based on consent. |

---

### **11. Data Transfers**

Nzila Ventures may transfer personal data across borders, including to countries outside the European Union (EU) or European Economic Area (EEA), subject to the necessary safeguards and in compliance with applicable data protection laws, such as the **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)**.

---

Failure to comply with this **Data Privacy Policy** may result in disciplinary actions, including termination of employment or contracts for serious violations. Regular audits and monitoring will be conducted to ensure ongoing compliance with the policy.

---

### **13. Review and Updates**

This **Data Privacy Policy** will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to ensure compliance with evolving data protection laws and best practices.
