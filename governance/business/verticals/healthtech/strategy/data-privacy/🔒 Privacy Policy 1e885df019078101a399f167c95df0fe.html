1. Who We Are

Memora is a wellness platform designed to support cognitive routines, emotional encouragement, and caregiver connection for aging individuals and their care networks.

We are not a medical provider, and our platform does not replace professional health services.

  • Transparent data practices
  • Consent-based access and sharing
  • Canadian data residency
  • No commercial sale or reuse of your data

Memora is compliant with:

  • GDPR (where applicable for research exports)
