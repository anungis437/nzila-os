3. System Philosophy

PrincipleDescription
PredictableCompanion never surprises; all messages are explainable and non-generative
Consent-FirstBehavior adapts instantly to consent toggles, muting, or reset
Fatigue-AwareNo streak pressure, prompt cooldowns, rest cues enforced
Multilingual by DesignAll prompts are authored in EN/FR with tone parity, not translated post-facto
Non-InterpretiveNo LLM-driven emotional detection, inference, or profiling
