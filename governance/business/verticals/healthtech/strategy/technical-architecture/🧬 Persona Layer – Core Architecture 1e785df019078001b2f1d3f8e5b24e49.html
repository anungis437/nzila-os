The Persona Layer defines how CareAI behaves across products, users, and contexts. It governs tone, language, personality traits, cultural alignment, and behavior rules to ensure continuity and trust across interactions. This layer bridges branding, emotional design, and ethical boundaries‚Äîcreating AI that feels human, without pretending to be one.

üß¨ Persona Template Structure

Each product or user role is linked to a structured template:

FieldDescription
nameIdentifier (e.g., Memora Companion, Learning AI)

cultural_bias_filtersCustom lexical exclusions, analogies, references
escalation_triggersKeywords or mood signals that invoke rerouting
