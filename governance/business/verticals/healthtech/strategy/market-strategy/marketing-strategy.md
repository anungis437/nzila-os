# Marketing Strategy

**Owner:** Aubert

### üß† **Overview**

The **Memory Match Marketing Strategy** focuses on positioning the game as an accessible and engaging cognitive tool for a wide range of users, from those in clinical settings to everyday users seeking cognitive enhancement. The strategy aims to build awareness, foster user engagement, and drive growth through strategic partnerships, direct user acquisition, and community engagement. The game will be marketed with an emphasis on its cognitive benefits, ease of use, and integration into both clinical and personal routines.

---

### üéØ **Target Audience**

1. **Clinical Partners**
2. **Type**: Elderly care facilities, memory care units, rehabilitation centers, neurologists.
3. **Needs**: Cognitive stimulation, patient engagement, and tracking of cognitive health progress.
4. **Messaging**: Memory Match is a therapeutic, easy-to-integrate tool for improving cognitive function in elderly populations.
1. **Caregivers**
2. **Type**: Family caregivers, professional home caregivers.
3. **Needs**: Tools to aid in the cognitive stimulation of loved ones, stress relief, and emotional support.
4. **Messaging**: Memory Match offers caregivers a tool to engage and support loved ones with fun, easy-to-use cognitive games that foster growth and mental well-being.
1. **Healthcare Providers**
2. **Type**: Geriatric doctors, therapists, occupational therapists.
3. **Needs**: Cognitive health tools to enhance therapeutic interventions and long-term patient care.
4. **Messaging**: A scientifically-backed tool that can be incorporated into therapeutic routines to strengthen memory and improve patient outcomes.
1. **General Consumers**
2. **Type**: Individuals interested in maintaining cognitive health.
3. **Needs**: Cognitive wellness, improvement of short-term memory and cognitive function.
4. **Messaging**: An engaging way to keep your mind sharp through fun, relaxing, and scientifically designed games.

---

### üìä **Marketing Objectives**

1. **Increase Brand Awareness**

Position **Memory Match** as a trusted cognitive tool across key verticals including clinical settings, caregiving, and personal wellness.
1. **Drive User Acquisition**

Attract both clinical clients (elderly care centers, rehab clinics) and individual users through compelling offers and engagement strategies.
1. **Community Engagement & Advocacy**

Build a user community where people can share their experiences, tips, and feedback, driving loyalty and organic growth.
1. **Expand Premium User Base**

Increase conversion rates from freemium to premium users by showcasing the extended functionality and personalization options of the premium tier.

---

### üí° **Key Marketing Channels**

1. **Partnerships & B2B Marketing**
2. **Target**: Clinical partners, healthcare institutions, and memory care homes.
3. **Tactics**:
4. Co-branded promotions with healthcare institutions and clinical experts.
5. Offering a trial period for clinics to test Memory Match in their facilities.
6. Developing case studies from early adopters in clinical settings to showcase efficacy.
1. **Digital & Content Marketing**
2. **Target**: Caregivers, general consumers.
3. **Tactics**:
4. **SEO Optimization**: Create blog posts, articles, and resources on cognitive health, elderly care, and memory improvement.
5. **Influencer Marketing**: Partner with influencers in the caregiving space to review the game and raise awareness.
6. **Email Campaigns**: Regular email newsletters to promote new features, game tips, and user testimonials.
7. **Social Media**: Instagram, Facebook, Twitter ‚Äì promoting engaging content, user stories, and behind-the-scenes insights.
1. **Referral & Word-of-Mouth**
2. **Target**: General consumers, caregivers, and healthcare providers.
3. **Tactics**:
4. Referral incentives (e.g., free premium access for successful referrals).
5. Encouraging users to share their results on social media, linking their cognitive health achievements to the game.
1. **In-App Marketing**
2. **Target**: Existing freemium users.
3. **Tactics**:
4. In-game promotions encouraging users to upgrade to premium for additional levels and content.
5. Notifications about limited-time offers or discounts for premium memberships.

### üèÜ **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**

1. **Brand Awareness**
2. Website traffic (via organic search, referral traffic, and paid ads).
3. Social media impressions, likes, shares, and comments.
4. Press coverage and media mentions.
1. **User Acquisition**
2. Number of new users who download and start playing the game.
3. Freemium-to-premium conversion rate.
4. Number of clinical partners signed on.
1. **Community Engagement**
2. Active community participation (forum, social media, user-generated content).
3. User testimonials and positive reviews on app stores.
1. **Revenue Growth**
2. Monthly active users (MAUs) on premium subscriptions.
3. Monthly recurring revenue (MRR) from premium users.
4. Conversion rate from freemium to premium users.
