Memora does not define success by daily logins or time spent in-app. Instead, we track:
- **Sustained, emotionally safe use**
- **Relational consistency, not performance**
- **Support ecosystem alignment** (patient + caregiver + clinic)

These metrics support evaluations by:
- Clinics (pilot retention & adherence)
- Researchers (behavioral insight & fatigue response)
- Funders (impact reporting & ROI)
- Internal product teams (Companion tuning & feature roadmap)
