3. Customer Segmentation

A. B2B Clients

  • Profiles: Healthcare orgs, clinics, care homes, education partners
  • Success Path:
    • High-touch onboarding
    • Executive sponsor check-ins
    • Usage dashboards + ROI tracking
    • Expansion playbooks for scaling across teams/sites

B. B2C Users (PuzzleLoop, Cognition Passport)

  • Profiles: Consumers, caregivers, tech-savvy families
  • Success Path:
    • In-app onboarding
    • Gamified goal tracking and nudges
    • Email drip series with usage tips
    • Reward systems and content unlocks

6. Success Infrastructure

  • Customer Success Managers (CSMs) for B2B:

    Strategic partners who own onboarding, success planning, renewals, and escalation.

  • In-app Success Automation for B2C:

    Journey mapping, nudges, tooltips, and AI-driven suggestions embedded in PuzzleLoop, etc.

  • Success Toolkit (Cross-product):
    • Onboarding playbooks
    • ROI calculators
    • Persona-based value propositions
    • Case study templates
    • Email/drip content library
    • Customer Health Dashboards (integrated with product and CRM data)
  • Feedback Channels:
    • Quarterly NPS
    • Beta communities
    • In-app surveys
    • Feature request boards

7. Customer Advocacy & Voice Programs

  • Voice of the Customer (VoC) program to regularly synthesize feedback across studios
  • Customer Advisory Boards (CABs) for key B2B clients (CareAI, Memora)
  • Ambassador Program for B2C (PuzzleLoop), including rewards for testimonials, reviews, and referrals
  • Spotlight Stories: Publish client wins on website, blog, and social channels

8. Governance & Alignment

  • Quarterly Success Reviews

    Led by CSMs and Product, involving leadership review of customer portfolio health.

  • Studio Coordination

    Each venture has a designated Success Lead embedded into planning, reporting into central Customer Office.

  • Data Flow

    Customer health data flows bi-directionally between CRM, product analytics, and success tools to ensure unified insight across customer journeys.
