### 4. üéØ OKR Framework & Flow

| Level | Scope | Owner | Limit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Company-Level** | Cross-product impact, revenue, mission | CEO | Max 5 |
| **Product-Level** | Outcomes for Memora, Optiva, CareAI, etc. | CPO + PMs | Max 3 per product |

| **Contributor Goals** | Sprint-linked deliverables | Team members | Defined via kickoff |

> All OKRs are SMART: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound
