## üß± Core Operational Philosophy

Nzila operates on the following execution principles:
1. **Clarity over bureaucracy** ‚Äì Light documentation, high transparency
1. **Sprints over slogs** ‚Äì Small, fast cycles with clear scope and review
1. **No system without a steward** ‚Äì Every platform, doc, and folder has an owner
1. **Foundational before flashy** ‚Äì Infrastructure wins long-term
1. **Ops = Culture** ‚Äì How we operate *is* how we build culture
