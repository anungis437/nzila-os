üõ†Ô∏è Monetization Models

  1. Freemium Model:
    • Overview: Provides a free-to-play core experience with optional in-game purchases and upgrades. It includes essential features and games for patient engagement in clinical settings.
    • Revenue Source: In-app purchases (for premium content) and upgrades to premium games.
    • Target Audience: Casual players, caregivers, and clinics with limited budgets or those looking for basic engagement tools.
  1. Premium Model:
    • Overview: A subscription-based model that offers access to the full suite of Memory Match games, personalized content, exclusive themes, and additional features.
    • Revenue Source: Recurring subscription payments for accessing premium game sets and functionalities.
    • Target Audience: Healthcare institutions, senior care homes, research facilities, and families looking for comprehensive cognitive engagement solutions for long-term use.
  1. B2B Licensing & Partnerships:
    • Overview: Partnering with healthcare providers, clinics, insurance companies, and tech platforms to license Memory Match as part of a broader digital health offering.
    • Revenue Source: Licensing fees, volume-based discounts for large-scale deployments, and partner incentives.
    • Target Audience: Health networks, care providers, insurance companies, and pharmaceutical companies integrating cognitive wellness into their patient care plans.
  1. Data Monetization & Insights:
    • Overview: Providing anonymized, aggregated cognitive data insights to research institutions, healthcare organizations, and pharmaceutical companies working on cognitive health and dementia research.
    • Revenue Source: Selling anonymized data sets and research insights in compliance with privacy and consent regulations.
    • Target Audience: Research institutions, academic researchers, pharmaceutical companies developing cognitive health therapies.
  1. Donations & Sponsorships:
    • Overview: Attracting donations from non-profit organizations, philanthropic investors, and corporate sponsorships to fund access for low-income individuals, clinics, or regions with limited access to cognitive health tools.
    • Revenue Source: Charitable contributions, grant funding, and corporate sponsorship for specific programs.
    • Target Audience: Non-profit health organizations, philanthropic investors, and corporations with a social responsibility agenda.
