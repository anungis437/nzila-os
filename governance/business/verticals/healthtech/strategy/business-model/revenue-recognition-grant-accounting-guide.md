This guide ensures that **revenue and grant income** across all Nzila ventures is:

### ðŸ”¹ 2. Revenue Streams Overview

| Stream | Description | Recognition Rule |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **SaaS Licensing** | Revenue from clinics, B2C users, or platforms using Companion Engine | Recognized monthly over service period |
| **Pilot Participation Fees** | Payments from clinics/research partners for trial access | Recognized upon signed agreement + delivery start |
| **Product Bundles** | Revenue from multi-month pre-purchase | Deferred and recognized monthly |
| **Grants (Milestone-Based)** | Conditional grant disbursements (e.g. IRAP, AGE-WELL) | Recognized upon milestone approval |
| **Grants (Unrestricted)** | Lump-sum funding with flexible use | Recognized on receipt, unless donor specifies timeline |

### ðŸ”¹ 3. Recognition Method by Venture Stage

| Stage | Revenue Tracked? | Recognition Timing |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Discovery** | No revenue expected | N/A |
| **MVP Development** | Pilot interest logged only | Deferred (if prepayments made) |
| **Pilot Launch** | Recognize per milestone and delivery logs | Upon completion of delivery unit |
| **Commercialization** | Monthly recurring revenue (MRR) recognized | Pro-rata across subscription term |

### ðŸ”¹ 7. Compliance & Internal Review

| Risk Area | Mitigation |
| --- | --- |
