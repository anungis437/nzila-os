### Market Timing Catalysts

- **Bill 67 (Ontario):** Mandated anti-racism training for 1.2M public sector employees — phased enforcement begins 2026-2027
- **ESG Reporting Deadlines:** TSX-listed companies required to disclose diversity metrics starting fiscal 2026
- **CHRC Federal Mandate:** Federal employers (100+ employees) must demonstrate anti-racism training compliance
- **AODA Convergence:** Accessibility + DEI training requirements increasingly overlap in procurement RFPs

| Competitor | Market Cap / Revenue | Strengths | Weaknesses vs ABR Insights |

### Competitive Moat

1. **10,000+ CanLII tribunal cases** — only platform with searchable, AI-indexed anti-racism case law
2. **200+ Companion Prompts** (copyright registered, trade secret) — proprietary GPT-4 prompt library
3. **Gamification engine** — 80%+ completion rate vs 25-30% industry average (18-table engine)
4. **CE credit partnerships** — CPHR (28K+ members), OAHPP (6K+ health professionals)
5. **Bill 67 first-mover** — purpose-built for Ontario mandate compliance
