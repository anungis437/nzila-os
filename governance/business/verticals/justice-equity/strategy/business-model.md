## 1. Revenue Streams

| Stream | Type | Contribution (2026) | Contribution (2030) |
|--------|------|--------------------|--------------------|
| SaaS Subscriptions | Recurring (ARR) | 70% | 55% |
| Seat Expansion | Recurring (Expansion) | 10% | 20% |
| Add-On Course Packages | Recurring/One-time | 8% | 12% |
| CE Credit Bundles | Recurring | 7% | 8% |
| Consulting Services | One-time/Retainer | 5% | 5% |

### Stream Details

**SaaS Subscriptions:** Core platform access — LMS, tribunal database, GPT-4 coach, gamification engine, admin dashboards. Priced per-seat annually.

**Seat Expansion:** Organic growth within existing accounts. Average account grows from 50 → 100 seats within 12 months as departments adopt beyond initial team.

**Add-On Course Packages:** Sector-specific course bundles (healthcare ABR, legal profession ABR, education sector ABR). Priced at $2,000-$3,000 per bundle.

**CE Credit Bundles:** CPHR, OAHPP, Law Society CPD credits bundled with course completion. $400-$500 per learner per CE program.

**Consulting Services:** DEI strategy audits, custom course development, implementation support. $10,000-$25,000 per engagement.
