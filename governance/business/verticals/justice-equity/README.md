## üèÜ FLAGSHIP: ABR INSIGHTS

### **Product Overview**

**ABR Insights** is Canada's first AI-powered Anti-Black Racism learning platform, combining an LMS (Learning Management System), tribunal case database (10,000+ CanLII cases), GPT-4 learning coach, gamification engine, and continuing education (CE) credits (CPHR, OAHPP accredited).

- **Database Entities:** 132 Supabase tables

- **Gamification Engine:** 18 tables (XP, badges, leaderboards, streaks, study buddies, adaptive progression curve)

- **Tech Stack:** Next.js 14+ App Router, Azure PostgreSQL, Drizzle ORM, Clerk auth (Profile: nextjs-aca-azurepg-clerk)

### **Regulatory & Compliance Drivers**

**Ontario Bill 67 (2023):**
- **Mandate:** Mandatory anti-racism training for Ontario public sector (schools, hospitals, municipalities, provincial government)
- **Scope:** 1.2M public sector employees (teachers, nurses, civil servants, police, transit workers)
- **Timeline:** Phased rollout (2024-2026), full compliance by 2027
- **ABR Insights Positioning:** Pre-configured Bill 67 compliance module (training certificates, annual refresher courses, reporting dashboards)

**AODA (Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act):**
- **DEI Requirement:** Accessibility training must include anti-racism, anti-Black racism, Indigenous cultural competency
- **Scope:** All Ontario organizations (private + public), 15,000+ medium/large enterprises
- **ABR Insights Positioning:** AODA-compliant module (accessible UX, screen reader support, closed captions, keyboard navigation)

**CHRC (Canadian Human Rights Commission):**
- **Federal Mandate:** Anti-Black racism systemic reviews (federal departments, crown corporations, federally regulated industries)
- **Scope:** Banking, telecoms, airlines, railways, federal public service (500K employees)
- **ABR Insights Positioning:** Federal procurement vendor (CHRC-aligned curriculum, tribunal case analysis, government reporting)

**ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) Reporting:**
- **TSX/NYSE Requirements:** Public companies disclose diversity metrics (board composition, workforce demographics, DEI training hours)
- **Scope:** 3,500+ TSX-listed companies, 2,800+ NYSE-listed Canadian companies
- **ABR Insights Positioning:** ESG reporting dashboards (training hours, completion rates, diversity impact metrics)
