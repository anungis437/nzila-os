# EdTech-Cybersecurity — Market Strategy

> Market analysis and go-to-market strategy for CyberLearn — cybersecurity training LMS for SMBs and MSPs.

---

## Market Overview

### Total Addressable Market
- **Global cybersecurity training**: $18B (2024) → projected $44B by 2030 (16% CAGR)
- **Canadian cybersecurity market**: $4.2B (training segment ~$600M)
- **SMB security awareness training**: $3.5B globally
- **MSP-delivered security training**: $1.2B (fastest-growing segment)
- **TAM**: $18B (global security training)
- **SAM**: $600M (Canadian + North American SMB/MSP training)
- **SOM**: $80K Year 1 → $500K Year 3 → $2M Year 5

### Market Dynamics
- Cyber insurance now requires security awareness training (driving demand)
- 82% of breaches involve human element (Verizon DBIR 2024)
- MSPs increasingly bundle security training with managed services
- Canadian government mandating cybersecurity for critical infrastructure (Bill C-26)
- Skills gap: 3.5M unfilled cybersecurity jobs globally
- Compliance-driven: PCI-DSS, PIPEDA, SOC 2 all require security training programs

### Canadian/MSP-Focused
| Platform | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---|---|---|
| **Beauceron Security** | Canadian, bilingual, MSP-friendly | Smaller content library |
| **Terranova Security** | Montreal-based, privacy-focused content | Enterprise pricing |
| **CyberSmart** | UK-based, certification included | Not in Canadian market |

### CyberLearn Differentiation
- **MSP-first**: Multi-tenant architecture designed for MSP channel
- **Canadian compliance**: PIPEDA, Bill C-26, provincial privacy law content built-in
- **Bilingual**: Full French + English content library (not just translated UI)
- **Affordable SMB pricing**: 60% less than KnowBe4 for <100 user organizations
- **Simulated environments**: Hands-on labs, not just videos + quizzes
- **Gamification**: Leaderboards, badges, team competitions (engagement driver)

## Target Segments

### Tier 1 — Managed Service Providers (MSPs)
- **Size**: 5,000+ MSPs in Canada, 40,000+ in North America
- **Behavior**: Bundle security training with managed security services
- **Value**: Multi-tenant management, white-label reporting, bulk licensing
- **ARPU**: $200-2,000/mo per MSP (depends on client count)
- **Channels**: MSP conferences (IT Nation, DattoCon), peer groups (HTG, ConnectWise)

### Tier 2 — SMBs Direct (50-500 employees)
- **Size**: 100,000+ Canadian SMBs with 50-500 employees
- **Behavior**: Driven by cyber insurance requirements, compliance needs
- **Value**: Easy setup, compliance reporting, phishing simulation included
- **ARPU**: $100-400/mo
- **Channels**: Insurance brokers, accounting firms, industry associations

### Tier 3 — Professional Services Firms
- **Size**: 15,000+ law firms, accounting firms, financial advisors in Canada
- **Behavior**: High-value data targets, regulatory obligation for security training
- **Value**: Industry-specific content (legal, financial), compliance certificates
- **ARPU**: $150-300/mo
- **Channels**: CPA Canada, Law Society chapters, professional association partnerships

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1 — MSP Channel Build (Q3-Q4 2025)
- Launch with 10 MSP beta partners in Ontario
- Provide white-label option: MSP brand on training portal
- Offer first 3 months free for beta MSPs (exchange for feedback)
- Channels: MSP meetups (Toronto, Ottawa), ConnectWise community
- Goal: 10 MSPs, 500 end-users, $5K MRR

### Phase 2 — Canadian MSP Scale (2026)
- Expand to 50 MSP partners across Canada
- Launch phishing simulation engine
- French content library for Quebec MSPs
- Partnership: major PSA/RMM vendors (ConnectWise, Datto, NinjaRMM)
- Goal: 50 MSPs, 5,000 end-users, $30K MRR

### Phase 3 — Direct SMB + US Expansion (2027)
- Direct sales to SMBs (insurance broker channel)
- US market entry via MSP partner network
- Industry-specific content packs (healthcare, legal, financial, manufacturing)
- Goal: 100 MSPs + 200 direct SMBs, 25,000 users, $100K MRR

### Phase 4 — Platform Leadership (2028)
- Cyber range: advanced hands-on lab environment
- Certification program: CyberLearn Certified Security Aware Professional
- Enterprise tier: SOC 2 / ISO 27001 training modules
- Goal: 500 MSPs/SMBs, 100,000 users, $300K MRR
