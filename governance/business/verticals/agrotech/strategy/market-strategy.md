## 3. GTM Playbook

### Phase 1: DRC Cooperatives (2025-2026)
**Objective**: Establish beachhead in DRC through cooperative networks

- **Target**: 45 cooperatives across Kinshasa, Bas-Congo, and Katanga provinces
- **Entry point**: Cooperative federation leadership — top-down adoption through federation endorsement
- **Onboarding model**: Field agent teams (3-person squads) deployed per province
- **Product lead**: PonduOps cooperative management → CORA marketplace upsell
- **KPI**: 85% cooperative retention at 6 months, 500+ active farmers per cooperative

### Phase 2: East Africa Expansion (2027-2028)
**Objective**: Replicate DRC model in Rwanda and Uganda

- **Target**: 80 cooperatives in Rwanda (coffee/tea), 50 in Uganda (grain/horticulture)
- **Entry point**: Rwanda Agriculture Board partnership, Uganda cooperative unions
- **Localization**: Kinyarwanda and Luganda language packs, local payment integrations (MTN Mobile Money)
- **Product lead**: CORA cross-border supply chain matching (DRC ↔ Rwanda trade corridors)
- **KPI**: 60-day cooperative activation, $15K+ ARR per enterprise contract

### Phase 3: Pan-African (2029-2030)
**Objective**: Platform network effects across 5+ countries

- **Target**: Kenya, Tanzania, Ghana — high-agtech-adoption markets
- **Entry point**: Regional trade organizations (COMESA, EAC), multi-country NGO programs
- **Product lead**: CORA marketplace GMV driving transaction fee revenue
- **KPI**: $980K ARR, 700+ cooperatives, 50K+ active farmers

## 6. Customer Acquisition & CAC Optimization

### Acquisition Channels (by CAC)
1. **Cooperative federation referrals**: $30 CAC — federation endorsement drives batch adoption
2. **Field agent direct onboarding**: $80-120 CAC — 3-person teams, in-person training
3. **Government program integrations**: $45 CAC — subsidized adoption through government contracts
4. **NGO-facilitated pilots**: $60 CAC — grant-funded deployments with built-in farmer cohorts
5. **Digital (SMS/radio campaigns)**: $150 CAC — rural radio ads + SMS registration funnels

### CAC Optimization Tactics
- **Cooperative multiplier effect**: Onboarding 1 cooperative leader activates 50-500 farmers (effective per-farmer CAC: $0.24-$2.40)
- **Train-the-trainer model**: Cooperative managers become onboarding agents, reducing field team costs by 60%
- **Government subsidy stacking**: Layer agricultural development grants to offset first-year subscription costs
- **Referral incentives**: Cooperative-to-cooperative referral bonus (1 month free per successful referral)
- **Seasonal timing**: Align onboarding campaigns with pre-planting season when farmers are planning

### Blended CAC Target
- CORA: $120 → optimize to $85 by 2027 through federation channel scaling
- PonduOps: $100 → optimize to $65 by 2027 through cooperative multiplier effect
- Maintaining LTV/CAC ratios above 90x as CAC decreases and retention improves
