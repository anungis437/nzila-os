### 2. **Validation Framework**

Nzila applies a **3-stage validation model** across ventures:

| **Stage** | **Focus** | **Key Tests & Indicators** |
| --- | --- | --- |

| **2. Solution Fit** | Does the product work as expected? | Prototypes, usability tests, proof-of-value pilots |
| **3. Market Fit** | Is there strong demand and retention? | Activation rates, DAU/MAU, referral, NPS, qualitative ‚Äúpull‚Äù |

### 6. **Iteration & Kill Criteria**

Nzila enforces a **disciplined iteration loop**:

- **If** PMF KPIs aren‚Äôt met after 2 pilot cycles or > 3 iterations,
- **Then**:
    - Pivot scope
    - Reframe the core problem
    - Or **retire the product** if validation consistently fails

This ensures capital and effort are reallocated toward ventures with measurable traction and transformative potential.

---

### 7. **Responsible PMF Practices**

- Never force engagement through manipulation (e.g., dark UX or anxiety-driven design)
- Always **return control to the user** (e.g., memory opt-outs, rest states)
- Consider PMF only valid if it **includes underserved users**, not just tech-privileged audiences
- Apply **privacy, consent, and ethics reviews** at every feature validation stage
