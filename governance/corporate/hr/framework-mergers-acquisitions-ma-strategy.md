### 5. **Due Diligence Framework**

Nzila will adopt a **3-lens diligence approach**:

### 1. **Strategic & Cultural Fit**

- Vision compatibility, product roadmap overlap
- Ethics and DEI practices
- Leadership and team retention feasibility

### 2. **Technical & Operational Audit**

- Code quality, scalability, security posture
- Data ownership, licensing, AI/ML integrity
- Vendor dependencies, DevOps readiness

### 3. **Financial & Legal Health**

- Revenue quality (if applicable), growth history
- IP assignments, past disputes, regulatory compliance (e.g. HIPAA, GDPR)
- Liabilities, equity structure, exit rights
