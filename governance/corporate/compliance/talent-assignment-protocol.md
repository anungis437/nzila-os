**How Nzila deploys internal and fractional talent across ventures**

---

### **1. Purpose**

This protocol ensures:

- Strategic, **milestone-tied deployment** of shared team members (engineering, product, content, etc.)
- Clear rules for **availability, time allocation, and cost tracking**
- **Non-competitive access** across ventures based on readiness, equity stage, and compliance
- Transparent **governance of fractional, contract, and full-time resource use**

---

---

### **3. Assignment Criteria**

| Criteria | Requirement |
| --- | --- |
| **Milestone Readiness** | Must have completed latest milestone in Strategic Review |
| **Capital Clarity** | Must have allocated ledgered capital or forecasted burn |
| **IP License Active** | Team must be building on licensed Nzila infrastructure |
| **No SLA Breaches** | Ventures with >2 unclosed SLA requests are deprioritized |

### **7. Conflict & Prioritization Rules**

| Conflict Type | Resolution Rule |
| --- | --- |
| Multiple ventures request same resource | Priority to nearest active pilot or revenue milestone |
