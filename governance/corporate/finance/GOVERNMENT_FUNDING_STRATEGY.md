1. ‚úÖ **Project Title** (e.g., "Machine Learning Algorithm for Union Pension Fund Forecasting")

   - What new knowledge was generated?
   - How does it advance the field?
4. ‚úÖ **Systematic Investigation** (Hypothesis Testing)

   - Results documented
5. ‚úÖ **Qualified Personnel** (engineers, data scientists with relevant expertise)
6. ‚úÖ **Time & Cost Allocation**
   - Hours per project (from Jira/Harvest)
   - Salary allocation (prorated)

- [ ] Literature review (academic papers, competitor analysis showing gaps)
- [ ] Time tracking (Jira tickets, Harvest logs, >80% coverage)
- [ ] Financial allocation (separate R&D accounting codes in QBO)

**Nzila Process**:
