### ğŸ”¹ 2. Spinout Definition

A **spinout** refers to the creation of a new legal corporation (federally or provincially incorporated) that:

- Operates a product originated under Nzila's venture studio model
- May raise third-party capital under its own cap table
- Licenses IP, brand elements, or backend systems from Nzila
- Is governed via a **venture-level Operating Agreement** + a **license agreement**
