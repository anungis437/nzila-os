### ðŸ”¹ 2. Role Definitions

| Role | Description | Applies To |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Founder (Nzila)** | Individual(s) who created Nzila, initiated its IP model, and maintain governing control over portfolio strategy | Nzila only |
| **Venture CEO** | Appointed lead for day-to-day management of a specific product (e.g., Memora), accountable to Nzila Board | Memora, Optiva |
| **Product Co-Founder (if designated)** | Recognized early contributor to a venture; may receive equity or options but not control IP or legal structure | Venture-level only |
| **Nzila Executive (CXO)** | Core leadership at the parent level (e.g., Head of Product Ops, Legal, Strategy) | Nzila only |

### ðŸ”¹ 3. Authority & Decision Rights

| --- | --- | --- |

### ðŸ”¹ 5. Founder & Executive Rights (Safeguards)

| Provision | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| **Reverse Vesting for Founders (Nzila)** | Ensures continued commitment; vests over 4 years unless otherwise agreed |
| **Non-Compete + NDA** | Applies to all founders, execs, and advisors across ventures |
| **Board Observer Option** | Founders can be granted observer role on venture boards (non-voting) unless conflict of interest exists |
| **Vesting Cliff (Exec Roles)** | Executives must pass 1-year cliff for full equity vesting at both HQ and venture levels |

### ðŸ”¹ 6. Founder Participation in Spinouts

| Criteria | Notes |
| --- | --- |
| Must be actively leading or contributing >20% FTE | To hold meaningful equity in the spun-out venture |
| Must assign any contributed IP to Nzila before vesting | No dual-ownership of code, models, or designs |
| Must sign standard Founder Participation Agreement | Includes clawback clause and governance clause |
| Cannot independently raise capital for venture without approval | Fundraising must be coordinated via Nzila HQ |
